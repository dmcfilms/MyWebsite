About

Dakar perspective. Global visual language.

DMC Films is led by Djim Mouhameth Cisse, a motion designer, VFX artist and video editor based in Dakar, Senegal. The studio focuses on storytelling, rhythm, design and cinematic visuals to help clients communicate with clarity and emotion.
